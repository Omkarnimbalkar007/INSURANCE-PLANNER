Features

Room rent limit
Up to single private room permissible for hospitalization in this plan
Why is it Important
A lower cap is better as room rent eats into the claim amount which should be spent on treatment cost.
Restoration of cover
Reinstatement of Rs 7.5 Lacs for a non-related illness if the original sum insured gets exhausted in a year
Why is it Important
It helps at the time of second hospitalization in the same year
